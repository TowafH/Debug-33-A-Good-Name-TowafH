Animals!


Elephant

The Elephant is the largest land mammal on earth, having massive bodies, large ears, and long trunks.

Hippo

A large semi-aquatic mammal that has a large barrel-shaped body, short legs, a short tail and an enormous head!

Flamingo

A type of wading bird that has slender legs, long, graceful necks, large wings, and short tails

Panda

A mammal that has a body typical of bears. It has black fur on its ears, eye patches, muzzle, legs, and shoulders.

Penguin

The largest of all living penguin species, the emperor penguin has a black head, chin, and throat, with broad yellow patches.
